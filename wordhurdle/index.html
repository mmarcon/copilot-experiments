WordHurdle

How to play

The goal of the game is to find as many words as possible using the letters provided.

  • To win the game, you have to find at least 5 words.
  • Words must be at least 4 letters long.
  • All letters can be used more than once.
  • If you can't fine any more words, you can throw the dice for new vowels.
  • Dice can be rolled only twice in the course of a game.
